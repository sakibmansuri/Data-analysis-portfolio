##Retail Sales Analysis

## BUSINESS QUESTIONS
1. Which product category generates the most revenue?
2. Which product sells the most units?
3. Which customers spend the most?

## KEY FINDINGS
1. Electronics generates the highest revenue, suggesting strong demand for higher priced items.
2. Clothing sells the most units but generates less revenue per item.
3. The average transaction value indicates typical purchase size.
4. Male and female customers contribute similar revenue, indicating balanced purchasing behavior.
5. Customers aged 25 to  40 generate the most revenue.
6. A small number of customers account for a large share of spending.
7. Electronics has the highest average price per item.
8. High-value purchases are dominated by electronics products.
